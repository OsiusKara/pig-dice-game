Game rules:

  • ❇️ The game has 2 players, playing in rounds

  • ❇️ In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his Current score

  • ❇️ BUT, if the player rolls a 1, all his Current score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn

  • ❇️ The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his Current score gets added to his GLOBAL score       Then it's the next player's turn

  • ❇️ The first player to reach 100 points on GLOBAL score wins the game

Good Luck 🙂
